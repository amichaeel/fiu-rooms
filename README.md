# FIU Rooms

Welcome to FIU Rooms, the intuitive platform designed specifically for students at Florida International University. Our mission is simple: to help you find empty classrooms on campus with ease. Whether you're looking for a quiet study space, a place to collaborate on group projects, or just a spot to relax between classes, FIU Rooms has got you covered.

## Features

- **Real-Time Classroom Availability:** Access up-to-date information on which classrooms are currently empty.
- **Campus-Wide Coverage:** Find available spaces across the entire campus, including all major buildings and faculties.
- **Scheduling Ahead:** Plan your day by checking classroom availability for future dates and times.
- **Mobile Friendly:** Access FIU Rooms on-the-go from any mobile device.

## Getting Started

To start using FIU Rooms, simply visit [fiu-rooms.vercel.app](fiu-rooms.vercel.app from your preferred web browser. No sign-up or login required—finding an empty classroom is just a few clicks away!
